Future of Adventure Sports in Boosting Indian Economy

Introduction

Adventure travel has been the newest trend within the tourism economy. It may be the natural inclination of exploration, or awareness of options globally thanks to social media, whatever be the reason, adventure travel is truly a novel way to experience new places. This, as a result, has contributed to adventure sports’ rising recognition in India.

India is blessed with a huge diversity of geographic landscapes, from rivers to beaches to mountains to lakes and beyond. This makes it the perfect location for a variety of adventure sports. Trekking and rafting thrive amidst the Himalayas’ majestic peaks and the numerous rivers that run through the area. Bungy jumping in Rishikesh has catapulted Rishikesh to the status of the ‘adventure capital of India’.

The effects of the pandemic on the market for adventure sports

The coronavirus outbreak in 2020 caused a jolt in the segment. Challenges for the section included the pandemic-induced lockdown, social distance standards, an abrupt halt to foreign travel, and elevated safety concerns. The impact of the border sealing, the hospitality, and aviation industries, and other factors contributed to the segment’s revenue drop.

Impact of sports tourism on Indian economys

Governments are now recognising the benefit of creating a conducive environment to grow adventure sports. Goa Tourism Development Corporation (GTDC) has been actively promoting their latest addition: Bungy jumping over Mayem in North Goa. The locations that are a hotspot to extreme sports events gain significantly from sports tourism. This not only opens up working opportunities, but also provides a brand new experience for visitors.

Future scenario of adventure sports in India

Taking account of increased interest of people in such activities drives the government to take initiatives to promote the same, thereby supporting the growth of adventure sports. The segment’s attraction to travellers will also be boosted by the rising use of social media, as well as travel and video blogging. With their thrilling and adrenaline-pumping experiences, adventure sports is expected to become increasingly population. People long to connect with nature—another reason for fostering the sector’s growth. People prefer adventure sports as a way to refresh and be ready for their fast-paced futures.

Final takeaway!

Since a long time ago, adventure sports have appeared to be a passion and excitement for people globally, and today more buzz is being generated around these brand-new forms of experiential travel. And the cherry on the cake is that India is blessed with many exciting locations which as a result draw domestic and international tourists annually and add to the economy.
